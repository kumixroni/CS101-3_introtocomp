Interests and Disciplines

Formula 1

My true engagement with Formula 1 began when my girlfriend introduced me to the sport, and I quickly became captivated by its complexity. What fascinates me most is the relentless pursuit of engineering perfection, the strategic ballet of the pit wall, and the high-speed psychological challenge faced by the drivers. It's a sport that perfectly blends cutting-edge technology with high-stakes human performance, teaching me the value of efficiency and precision under intense pressure.

Making Coffee

My passion for coffee began as a simple curiosity, sparked by my sister in high school, but it truly developed into a craft when I worked as a barista during college. That experience immersed me in the science of brewing, teaching me the importance of controlling every variable from grind size and water temperature to extraction time. This hobby is now my morning ritual, where the meticulous process of crafting the perfect cup reinforces my professional commitment to consistency and attention to detail.

Gaming

Gaming has always been a central part of my life, evolving from a simple childhood iPad pastime to my primary source of digital entertainment on a custom PC. I enjoy a range of genres, particularly First-Person Shooters, Open World exploration, and Racing games. Whether I'm making split-second decisions in a competitive match or strategically navigating a vast landscape, gaming is my favorite way to sharpen my focus, test my problem-solving skills, and engage with compelling digital narratives.
